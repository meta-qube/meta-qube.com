How it works

Guided startup assembly

Each answer classifies the startup. Each classification selects modules. Each module adds structure. The system compiles the core and prepares executable business processes.

Classify

Start with a wizard: select what you're creating, ownership structure, domain, and launch model. Service, product, platform, marketplace, agency, software, AI, and more.

Assemble

Compatible meta-modules are selected automatically. Each module brings reviewed expert knowledge — inputs, outputs, constraints, and validation rules.

Compile

The Startup Compiler produces your full blueprint: business model, product structure, processes, architecture, legal drafts, pitch structure, and launch checklist.

Verify

Verifier agents check assumptions, compatibility, risks, and feasibility. Mentors and experts step in when human judgment is required.

Run

Startup Runtime executes business processes: lead capture, sales, payments, onboarding, support, and continuous operations — without operational gaps.

Grow

Prepare for pitch and fundraising. Evolve modules, pricing, and workflows while operations continue. Market feedback closes the loop.
